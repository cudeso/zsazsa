Writing a strong GIR

A General Intelligence Requirement captures an ongoing monitoring need rather than a one-time question. It should:

  • Define a monitoring area. Describe the threat landscape, sector, or actor group to track continuously.
  • Anchor it to stakeholders. Identify who benefits from this standing collection.
  • Set a review cadence. GIRs should be revisited periodically to confirm they remain relevant.
  • Plan the deliverable. Format and distribution should match the stakeholders' workflow.
  • Bound the scope. Geography, sectors, threat actors, threat types and exclusions keep collection focused.
