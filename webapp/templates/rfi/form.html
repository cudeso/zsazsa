Capturing a good RFI

An RFI is an ad-hoc question from a stakeholder. Capture enough context to triage and respond:

  • Who is asking, and for what decision? The same question from a SOC lead vs. a CISO has a different answer.
  • Tie it to a PIR or GIR if possible. Recurring RFIs hint at missing standing requirements.
  • Set a realistic due date. SLA hints: High {{ sla_days.High }}BD, Medium {{ sla_days.Medium }}BD, Low {{ sla_days.Low }}BD.
  • Always close the loop. Record the response and capture feedback to feed the metrics dashboard.
  • Attachments and notes can be added once the RFI is created.
