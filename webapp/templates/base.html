Intelligence Reference

Admiralty Scale · TLP · CTI Evaluation

Source Reliability
A
Completely reliable No doubt of authenticity or competency; history of complete reliability
B
Usually reliable Minor doubt; history of valid information most of the time
C
Fairly reliable Doubt of competency but has provided valid information in the past
D
Not usually reliable Significant doubt but has provided valid information in the past
E
Unreliable Lacking in trustworthiness and competency; history of invalid information
F
Cannot be judged No basis exists for evaluating the reliability of the source
Information Credibility
1
Confirmed Confirmed by other independent sources; consistent with other information
2
Probably true Not confirmed; logical in itself; consistent with other information
3
Possibly true Not confirmed; reasonably logical; agrees with some other information
4
Doubtful Not confirmed; possible but not logical; no other information on subject
5
Improbable Not confirmed; not logical in itself; contradicted by other information
6
Cannot be judged No basis exists for evaluating the validity of the information

A lower letter and a lower number indicate higher confidence. Scores are independent: a reliable source can still produce unconfirmed information.

Traffic Light Protocol (TLP)
TLP:RED
Individual recipients only - no further disclosure Cannot be effectively acted upon without significant risk. Recipients may not share with anyone else.
TLP:AMBER
Organization and its clients, need-to-know basis Requires support to act upon but carries risk if shared more widely. Add +STRICT to limit sharing to the organization only.
TLP:GREEN
Community - not for public channels Useful for awareness within the wider community. May be shared with peers and partners but not via publicly accessible channels.
TLP:CLEAR
No restrictions - may be shared publicly Carries minimal or no foreseeable risk of misuse. Subject to standard copyright rules, may be shared without restriction.
