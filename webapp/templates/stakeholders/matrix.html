Engagement guidance per quadrant
Manage Closely

Shapes the program's direction and acts on what they receive. Give them regular briefings, involve them in setting priority intelligence requirements, and respond quickly to their questions.

Keep Satisfied

Can affect the program even if they rarely engage with its output day to day. Keep them satisfied with periodic summaries and make sure nothing surprises them.

Keep Informed

Relies on the intelligence but has little say over the program itself. Keep them in the distribution lists for the products they care about and invite feedback.

Monitor

Currently low influence and low interest. Monitor for change rather than investing significant time, since a shift in their role or priorities can move them into another quadrant.
